# Career Launchpad · Day 2 — The AI Job & Freelance Market (2026)

Day 2 of the daily 30-min career track. Gives students the **map of opportunity** — real roles,
indicative pay, and where a beginner can realistically break in — so the next 43 days have a target.

## How to present it
Self-contained `index.html`. Double-click → **F** for fullscreen → **→ / Space** to advance,
**←** back, **Home/End** to jump. Works with a clicker.

## Slide flow + speaker notes
11 slides, ~28–30 min with discussion.

| # | Slide | Talk about / ask the room |
|--:|-------|---------------------------|
| 1 | Title | "Before you aim, know the target." |
| 2 | Good news | The skills gap = the opportunity. AI literacy is the new "knows Excel." |
| 3 | The roles | Map each role to a course phase so they see the through-line. |
| 4 | Job vs freelance | Both valid; many do both. Ask the room which appeals to them. |
| 5 | The money | ⭐ Indicative ₹ ranges — stress these vary by city/niche/proof, not a promise. |
| 6 | What they ask for | Read the pills; point out what's NOT needed (PhD, degree, 5 yrs). |
| 7 | SMB opportunity | The fastest realistic route to *first paid work*. Ties to capstone options. |
| 8 | Projects > years | The core mindset shift. "Show me something you built." |
| 9 | Where to look | Concrete platforms (India-relevant). They'll use these for the action. |
| 10 | **Career Action** | Give 5–8 min to actually find posts; share a few aloud. |
| 11 | Close | "Demand is real, supply is you." Tease Day 3. |

## Today's Career Action
**Find 5 real job/freelance posts** you'd want to land, and note the **skills each asks for**.
This becomes the target list we reverse-engineer your niche and content from.

> 💡 Tip: keep the list in a notes file — Days 16–18 (niche/positioning) will reuse it.
