# Career Launchpad · Day 1 — The Hidden Game

The **opening talk of the daily 30-min career track** (see [`CAREER-PLAN.md`](../../../CAREER-PLAN.md)).
Sets the whole thesis: skills get you *considered*, but **content + specialization** get you
*chosen*. Everything in the next 45 days builds on today.

## How to present it
Single self-contained `index.html` — no install. (Plus Jakarta Sans loads from Google Fonts online;
falls back cleanly offline.)
1. Double-click **`index.html`** (any browser).
2. Press **F** for fullscreen.
3. Navigate: **→ / Space / click-right** = next · **← / click-left** = back · **Home/End** = jump.

Works with a wireless clicker (maps to arrow keys). Progress bar on top, slide counter bottom-left.

## Slide flow + speaker notes
14 slides, ~28–30 min with discussion.

| # | Slide | Talk about / ask the room |
|--:|-------|---------------------------|
| 1 | Title | "By Day 45 you'll have an audience and a niche — not just a certificate." |
| 2 | The belief | Ask: "Who here thinks finishing this course = getting a job?" Let them commit, then challenge it. |
| 3 | The iceberg | Skills = visible tip. Proof/visibility/network = the 90% that decides hiring. |
| 4 | Two students | ⭐ The anchor story. Same skills, different visibility. Make it concrete. |
| 5 | Two levers | The thesis slide. Content + Specialization. Everything maps back here. |
| 6 | Lever 1: content | "Learn in public" — you don't need to be an expert. Posts work while you sleep. |
| 7 | Lever 2: niche | Read both pills aloud; ask which person they'd hire for a RAG job. |
| 8 | Trainer's story | Tell your *real* story here — how content/specialization got you work. Most authentic slide. |
| 9 | How the track runs | 30 min talk + one ≤15-min action, daily. |
| 10 | Compounding | The 1.01³⁶⁵≈37 math. One post ≈ nothing; 120 posts = a career asset. |
| 11 | Myth-busting | Address the objections in the room *before* they harden. Ask who relates. |
| 12 | What you'll walk out with | The payoff — presence, niche, portfolio, pipeline. |
| 13 | **Career Action** | Give them 5 min in-session to actually write the line. Collect a few aloud. |
| 14 | Close | Transition into the code (Module 01). Tease Day 2. |

## Today's Career Action
**Write one line:** *why you're really here, and where you want to be in 12 months.* (≤15 min.)
Keep it — it becomes the seed of your intro post on Day 7.
