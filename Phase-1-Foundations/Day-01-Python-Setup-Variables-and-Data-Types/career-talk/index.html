The whole track in one slide

Two levers that change everything

📣 1. Content / build in public

Show your work as you learn. Opportunities start coming to you instead of you chasing them.

🎯 2. Specialization

Be known for one specific thing. "I build RAG systems" beats "I know AI" every time.

Pull both levers for 45 days and you graduate as a known quantity, not a stranger with a certificate.

Lever 1

Content makes opportunity come to you

  • Every post is a tiny salesperson working for you 24/7 — even while you sleep.
  • Recruiters and clients search for people who post about their problem.
  • You don't need to be an expert — "learn in public" is enough. Teach what you learned yesterday.
  • A year of posts = an asset no one can take from you.
Lever 2

Specialists get picked first

When a company has a RAG problem, who do they hire — "an AI guy," or "the person whose whole feed is about RAG"?

❌ "I do AI / ML / web / apps" ✅ "I build document Q&A systems for legal teams"

Niche feels smaller, but it makes you the obvious choice — and you can always broaden later.
